Virtual reality control gloves

Technology companies, especially well-known names, are competing to develop the best and latest virtual reality glasses equipped with superior technical capabilities, while many startups are working in parallel to provide accessories that make it a truly exciting experience. After virtual reality shoes equipped with motion capture sensors, the new thing this time comes with virtual reality control gloves (Contact Gloves). The “Contact Gloves” is a (vr) controller in the form of a glove, equipped with tactile feedback and high-precision hand tracking.These controller-alternative gloves use tiny coils made of shape-memory alloys to provide tactile feedback powered by electronic circuitry, and consist of a tubular film that wraps around the finger and an alloy wrapped in shape-memory alloys. The use of shape-memory alloys allows the device to be more compact while reproducing a more realistic feel than current gloves that use vibration motors.

Who is the Syrian professor Osama Al-Khatib, winner of the Arab Geniuses 2024 award?
